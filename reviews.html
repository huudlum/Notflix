First Class By Name, First Class By Nature!


I really couldn’t recommend the setting enough. My son has been attending for almost two years now, and the preschool has gone above and beyond since day one. He initially started a couple of mornings a week before increasing to three days a week and then up to five days a week. He is excited to go to preschool every day; he loves spending time in the setting with his friends and the wonderful staff.
The setting provides so many enriching experiences for the children, from baking, preparing, and cooking food, to growing their own plants and introducing them to animals. The children have also had visits from local services (police, fire service, etc.) and other community groups. The preschool also arranges visits and activities for the children to learn about different cultural holidays and celebrations.
There is a wonderful garden for the children to explore, as well as the inside areas that are set up with different activities daily. There’s a great app that is regularly updated with the day’s activities, and the staff are happy to provide further updates at pick-up.
I’ll be sad in July when my son leaves the setting, but I honestly cannot praise First Class Preschool enough. The love, care, and expertise they have shown my son in the last two years is second to none. Alison, Jo, Tracey, and the team at First Class are amazing!
