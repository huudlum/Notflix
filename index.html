Positive & Confident

Our curriculum is child-focused. Learning starts the moment they enter the setting. They decide what they want to learn through predicting, inspecting, noticing, examining, testing, investigating, recording, discovering and exploring. We believe that a language-rich environment where each child is encouraged to interact and share thoughts and emotions builds confidence and self-worth.

Independent & Resilient

We believe in learning through play. Each childs developement should be driven by their interests and experiences to help build a foundation to future progress forward through both school and life. Authentic, open-ended resources and recyclable curiosities promotes natural play and imagination. However, we also make room for daily routines like welcome time and story time.

Valued & Respectful

We know each child is unique and strive to provide a fun and enabling environment in which every child can achieve the best start in their journey through education. We want to make sure that every child reaches their full potential and that no one gets left behind. Including parents. By building a strong partnership with you, we ensure that each child has a secure support network.
